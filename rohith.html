SILAMBAM

Silambam is an ancient Indian martial art originating from Tamil Nadu, renowned for its weapon-based combat, particularly the bamboo staff (kambu). It's considered one of the oldest martial arts in the world, with roots dating back to 1000 BC. Silambam is more than just a physical exercise; it incorporates techniques, discipline, respect, and spiritual practices.

A Silambam master is an expert practitioner and teacher of Silambam, a traditional Indian martial art from Tamil Nadu. They are skilled in various techniques involving bamboo sticks and other weapons, as well as bare-handed combat. Silambam masters often incorporate a focus on discipline, respect, and mental and physical well-being into their teaching.
